Turtle Categories

Turtles are an absolute beauty to watch! These creatures move with vanity and are very flamboyant with their beatiful shells and fins, which makes it blissfull for us to watch them. But, it's not everywhere on earth you can see these wonderful beings. However, Sri Lanka is blessed to have it's sea become a haven for sea turtles. Sri Lankan seas are home to 5 out of the 7 sea turtle types on earth. You will see blow these 5 types of sea turtles and some important facts about them.

Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

One of the most popular Turtle Hatcheries in Sri Lanka is, The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery. Here, You will find a brief description about the Hikkaduwa Hatchery and what you can do there. If you decide to pay a visit, you can find the location of the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery on this page.

The turtle hatchery in Hikkaduwa is the ideal location to see the endangered turtles that travel to Sri Lanka. By restoring them, the hatchery offers refuge to turtle eggs and damaged turtles. The hatchery gathers and holds the eggs until they are ready to hatch and return to the great blue in order to protect the number of turtles that come to the coastlines to nest. It is the ideal holistic experience for everybody since you could even get the opportunity to release some hatchlings back into the ocean.

The hatchery has multiple water tanks to host injured sea turtles, where they rehabilitate and send them back to the sea upon healing. They maintain safe water conditions so that the transition from the sea to the tank does not adversely impact them. They maintain separate environments to treat injured turtles, Help Mother Turtles' hatch eggs & to preserve newborn baby turtles until they're send back to the sea.

Here's a list of exciting things you can do if you decide to drop by at the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery:

  • Closely Observe Sea Turtles.
  • Get to know details about turtles from experts.
  • If you're lucky, release baby turtles into the sea.
  • And if you're keen, Sign up to be a volunteer!
