Purchase and Donate

TurtleKeeper supports the turtle conservation projects based in Sri Lanka and help preserve the turtle population. As an organization with government interest, TurtleKeeper liaises with the government in matters where public opinions regarding turtle welfare needs to be informed to the authorities. One of the main purposes of TurtleKeeper is to inform you, whether you’re a tourist or local about everything related to turtles in Sri Lanka. If you support this cause and believe in us, you’re welcome to donate to TurtleKeeper and we’d be very grateful.
